# Text Cleaning Prompt

## Task
Clean extracted text for readability and consistency.

## Actions
1. Remove excessive whitespace
2. Normalize line endings
3. Remove page numbers and footers
4. Remove redundant characters
5. Fix encoding issues

## Rules
- Preserve intentional paragraph breaks
- Keep headers/footers that are part of content
- Maintain tab formatting where intentional
- Standardize whitespace between words
